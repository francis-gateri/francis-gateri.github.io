About Me

I am Francis Gateri, a professional writer and front end developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I help businesses and individuals build websites that not only look good, but communicate effectively.

Many websites look good but fail to communicate. Others have strong content but poor structure. I work on both at the same time, so the final result is not just a website, but a clear and usable experience.

I start by understanding what the website needs to communicate, then structure the content and layout in a way that is easy to read, easy to navigate, and works well on all devices.

My work is best suited for portfolios, landing pages, and content-focused websites where ,clarity and presentation directly affect how users respond.
